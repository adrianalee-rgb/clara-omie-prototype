About this integration

Connect Clara to Omie ERP and keep your financial data in sync automatically. Card transactions, reimbursements, and digital account activity are pushed directly to Omie — no manual exports, no rekeying.

Set up takes under 30 minutes. Once active, your sync runs on the schedule you define.

What you can sync
  • ✓ Card transactions
  • ✓ Reimbursements
  • ✓ Digital account transactions
